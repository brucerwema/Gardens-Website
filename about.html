Our Mission

Cultivating Sustainable Gardens for Everyone

Founded in 2018, Gardens began with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to quality gardening supplies and expert knowledge, regardless of their experience level. What started as a small operation in Amsterdam has grown into a thriving community spanning Europe, Asia, and New Zealand.

We partner with local growers and sustainable suppliers to bring you carefully curated gardening boxes that reduce waste and promote biodiversity. Our oddly-shaped vegetables program alone has saved over 50,000 kilograms of produce from going to waste.

Today, we serve thousands of gardeners monthly, from complete beginners growing their first herbs to seasoned horticulturists cultivating elaborate wildflower meadows. Our commitment remains the same: make gardening accessible, sustainable, and joyful.
